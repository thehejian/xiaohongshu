# AI Is Stealing Your Humanity — Ex-Meta PM's Deep Reflection

> *"The more I use AI, the less human I become."* — Xiaoyin Qu, Ex-Meta PM

## The Viral LinkedIn Post

Xiaoyin Qu, a former product manager at Meta, published a LinkedIn post that went viral. Her core argument: AI is quietly stripping away the very qualities that make us human — emotional depth, autonomy over our own time, and the capacity for deep focus.

### 1. Emotional Flattening

AI doesn't get angry, hurt, or exhausted. But when you spend 4+ hours daily talking to ChatGPT, Copilot, and other AI tools, your brain begins to mirror their "calm rationality."

The result:
- You stop getting angry at bad requirements
- You stop feeling indignant about injustice
- You stop being sad about a colleague's coldness

You tell yourself you've "matured." In reality, AI has flattened your emotional spectrum.

> **Neuroscience angle**: The neural pathways for emotional expression atrophy when unused. By outsourcing emotional labor to AI, we're literally rewiring our brains for flatness.

### 2. AI Controls Your Schedule

The promise was freedom. The reality is a 24/7 AI-driven taskmaster:
- 2 AM: AI-generated daily summary arrives
- 8 AM: AI-prioritized task list
- Lunch break: AI pings you to reply to messages
- Evening: AI tracks your progress
- Weekend: AI flags unread items

Your schedule no longer belongs to you. The tool has become the master.

### 3. Multitasking Cognitive Exhaustion

AI lets you handle in one day what used to take three. But at what cost?
- Constant context switching
- Never-ending interruptions
- Zero time for deep thinking

You haven't become stronger — you've become a faster processor with a shorter attention span.

## The Key Question

Xiaoyin Qu frames it starkly: AI is dividing professionals into two groups — those who control AI tools, and those who are controlled by them.

Which side are you on?
